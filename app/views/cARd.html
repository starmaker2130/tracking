cARd
creative Augmented Reality desktop
the creative Augmented Reality desktop is an all purpose internet desktop built in augmented reality. Its interface allows users to treat the digital and real worlds as one continuous space, surpassing traditional constraints on creative collaboration.
cARds are designed to help us interact with the web more naturally. AR connects data to relevant real world objects to avoid overwhelming users as crowded, ad-supported web pages do. Now our hands are free to build digital experiences with the same intuition we use to create real ones
